I fundamentally disagree with the premise that automated curation will replace human editorial judgment entirely. The nuances of context are still completely missed by these models.

For example, in high-stakes environments like medical journalism or political reporting, an AI cannot weigh the ethical implications of surfacing a specific narrative over another. It only optimizes for engagement metrics. We need a hybrid approach, not a complete handover.
